How to Play

🎯 Goal

Win the big board by winning 3 small boards in a row.

🧩 Game Layout

The game is made of 9 small tic tac toe boards arranged in a 3×3 grid.

  • Each small board is a normal tic tac toe
  • The big board tracks which player wins each small board

🎮 How Moves Work

  • You place your mark (X or O) in a small cell
  • Your move decides where your opponent must play next
  • If you play in position 5, opponent must play in board 5

🔄 Special Case

If the target board is:

  • Already won OR
  • Completely full

👉 Then the next player can play anywhere

🏆 Winning

  • Win a small board → you claim that board
  • Win 3 small boards in a row → you win the game 🎉

🧪 Game Modes

  • Pass & Play → play locally on one device
  • Multiplayer → play online with a room code
  • Sandbox → no rules, just free play

💡 Quick Tip

Think ahead! Every move you make controls where your opponent plays next.
